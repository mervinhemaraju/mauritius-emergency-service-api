Privacy Policy

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any Personal Data.

The Mauritius Emergency Services app is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No tracking or analytics
  • No cookies or persistent identifiers
  • No location data collection
  • No contact information stored
  • No usage statistics collected

Data Sources

The Application displays information from two sources:

  • Emergency Contact Numbers: Manually curated and maintained by the Developer. These are publicly available contact numbers compiled from official sources and public directories.
  • Cyclone Information: Sourced from the Mauritius Meteorological Services website (http://metservice.intnet.mu/current-cyclone.php). Our API fetches this publicly available data and relays it to the Application for user convenience. We do not modify or alter this information.

How the Application Works

When you use the Application:

  1. The app connects to our API at https://mes.plagueworks.org/api
  2. The API provides emergency contact numbers (stored on our servers)
  3. The API fetches current cyclone information from the Mauritius Meteorological Services website
  4. This information is displayed to you in the app
  5. When you make a call, your device's native phone functionality is used
No data about your usage, calls, or interactions is sent back to us or stored anywhere.

Permissions

The Application may request the following permissions:

  • Phone/Call Permission: Required to initiate emergency calls directly from the app. This permission does not allow us to access your call history, contacts, or any other information.
  • Internet Access: Required to fetch emergency contact numbers and cyclone information from our API.

Data Storage

  • On Your Device: The app may temporarily cache data (emergency numbers, cyclone alerts) locally on your device for offline access. This data remains on your device only and is never transmitted to us.
  • On Our Servers: We maintain a database of emergency contact numbers for Mauritius. This database contains no user information—only publicly available emergency service contact details.

Third-Party Services

The Application relies on:

  • Our API (mes.plagueworks.org): Hosted and maintained by the Developer

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
